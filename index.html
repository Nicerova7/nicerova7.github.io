About

I am a Peruvian computer scientist with strong foundations in software engineering and applied machine learning. I graduated first in my class from the National University of Engineering in Lima, where I earned my B.Sc. in Computer Science. I currently work as a Machine Learning Engineer at RappiPay, developing predictive models for fintech applications such as fraud detection, delinquency prediction, and customer engagement.

My background includes research on robustness and generalization in computer vision, and I am now pursuing my passion for machine learning applied to audio—both as a standalone field and as part of multimodal AI systems. I have been fortunate to be advised by Nils Murrugarra-Llerena and Helder Rojas, whose mentorship has guided my early research direction.
